To help us resolve issues quickly, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro / iPad Air)
  • iOS/iPadOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • App version (shown in the app’s Settings/About screen, if available)
  • A brief description of what happened + steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots (optional but helpful)

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

Troubleshooting

  • App won’t load / blank screen: force close and reopen the app.
  • Content not updating: check your internet connection, then relaunch.
  • Purchase/subscription not recognized: use Restore Purchases (if available), then restart the app.
  • Unexpected behavior: restart your device and try again.

FAQ

How do I restore purchases?

If the app includes a Restore Purchases button, tap it while signed into the same Apple ID used for purchase.

Where can I report a bug or request a feature?

Email support@yourdomain.com with details.

Does the app provide medical advice?

No. The app is for educational/reference purposes only and does not replace professional medical judgment.
